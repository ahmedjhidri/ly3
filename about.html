About Allibiya Gas Company

Founded with a commitment to excellence, Allibiya Gases has grown into Libya's leading supplier of industrial and medical gases. With 24 years of experience, we operate 6 branches and produce 24,000 liters of oxygen daily, adhering to ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, and ISO 45001:2018 standards.

The company is equipped with the latest technologies and equipment, enabling it to provide high-quality products to its customers in various industrial and medical sectors. We are constantly striving to improve and enhance our operations and products to better meet the needs of customers and the market.
